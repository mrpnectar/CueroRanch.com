A Texas Gem

Discover Cuero Ranch, a stunning 52+ acre property 8 miles north of Cuero, TX, overlooking the Cuero Creek Valley. This custom-built 3BR/2BA home spans 2,441 sq ft, featuring limestone on all four sides, a standing seam metal roof, and inviting front porch, back porch, and cabana. The property includes a large metal shop with two roll-up doors (220V and 110V power), a spacious hay barn, and mature oak and pecan trees. The 52 acres are divided into thirds with cross-fencing for cattle, each section featuring accessible well water and a well-fed stock tank, all atop a scenic ridgeline.
